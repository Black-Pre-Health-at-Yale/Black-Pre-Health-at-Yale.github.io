Below are resources at Yale that can provide support for black students.

Yale League of Black Scientists

The Yale League of Black Scientists seeks to provide support in any way possible to any STEM student, regardless of race. They also can pair black undergraduates with a black graduate school mentor.

Yale League of Black Scientists

STARS

STARS is an undergraduate STEM research program committed to increasing minority representation in STEM. All underrepresented minority students are welcome to apply.
